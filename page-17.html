zinuru Festivials

Once a week, we gather a line-up of your favorite ZinUru Artists/Bands in a marvelous festival of uninterrupted music. Three hours of back to back shows, broadcasted to you wherever you are.

In this wonderous space of creativity and self-expression, you are all invited to enjoy it with old friends, make new friends, connect with our musicians, and have a blast!
